1. My Mental Model of the Geography

How I navigated Street View

I can't "walk" in Street View - arrow keys and click-to-move don't work via Chrome DevTools MCP. My workaround was:

  1. Search for a place or address on Google Maps (e.g., "Dodo Pizza Jana Pawla II")
  2. Click the "Street View" button on the place card to enter SV at that address
  3. Extract the panorama ID from the URL
  4. Navigate to the same panorama with different heading values (0=N, 90=E, 180=S, 270=W) to look in different directions
  5. To "move" to a new position, search for a different nearby address and repeat

This means I can only see Street View at locations where Google has a place/address with SV coverage. I can't explore freely between these points.
